# Calculus III

Multivariable calculus: partial derivatives, multiple integrals, vector calculus. The mathematical foundation for machine learning optimization (gradient descent, backpropagation) and physics simulations.

## Topics

### 1. [[math/calculus-3/partial-derivatives|Partial Derivatives]]
- Limits, Partial Derivatives, Higher Order
- Differentials, Chain Rule
- Directional Derivatives, Gradient

### 2. [[math/calculus-3/applications-partial|Applications of Partial Derivatives]]
- Tangent Plane, Normal Line
- Relative Extrema, Absolute Extrema
- Optimization, Lagrange Multipliers

### 3. [[math/calculus-3/multiple-integrals|Multiple Integrals]]
- Iterated Integrals, Double Integrals
- Double Integrals in Polar Coordinates
- Triple Integrals in Cylindrical/Spherical Coordinates
- Change of Variables, Surface Area

### 4. [[math/calculus-3/line-integrals|Line Integrals]]
- Vector Fields, Line Integrals (Arc Length, x/y, Vector Fields)
- Fundamental Theorem of Line Integrals
- Conservative Vector Fields, Potential Functions
- Green's Theorem, Curl, Divergence

### 5. [[math/calculus-3/surface-integrals|Surface Integrals]]
- Parametric Surfaces, Surface Integrals
- Surface Integrals of Vector Fields
- Stokes' Theorem, Divergence Theorem
