# Calculus II

Advanced integration techniques, sequences and series, parametric equations, and vectors. Critical for understanding probability distributions, signal processing, and numerical methods in ML.

## Topics

### 1. [[math/calculus-2/integration-techniques|Integration Techniques]]
- Integration by Parts
- Integrals Involving Trig Functions
- Trig Substitutions, Partial Fractions
- Integrals Involving Roots and Quadratics
- Integration Strategy, Improper Integrals
- Comparison Test, Approximating Definite Integrals

### 2. [[math/calculus-2/applications-integrals|Applications of Integrals]]
- Arc Length, Surface Area
- Center of Mass/Centroid
- Hydrostatic Pressure and Force, Probability

### 3. [[math/calculus-2/parametric-polar|Parametric Equations and Polar Coordinates]]
- Parametric Equations & Curves
- Calculus with Parametrics (Tangents, Areas, Arc Length, Surface Area)
- Polar Coordinates, Calculus with Polar Coordinates

### 4. [[math/calculus-2/sequences-series|Sequences and Series]]
- Sequences, Series, Convergence/Divergence
- Integral Test, Comparison Test, Limit Comparison Test
- Alternating Series Test, Ratio Test, Root Test
- Estimating Series Values, Power Series
- Taylor Series, Binomial Series

### 5. [[math/calculus-2/vectors-3d|Vectors and 3D Space]]
- Vector Basics, Magnitude, Unit Vector
- Dot Product, Cross Product, Projection
- 3D Coordinate System, Equations of Lines/Planes
- Quadratic Surfaces, Vector Functions
- Tangent/Normal/Binormal Vectors, Curvature
- Cylindrical Coordinates, Spherical Coordinates
