# The Recall Schedule

My forgetting curve is steeper than normal. I need MORE frequent recalls in the FIRST 24 HOURS.

| When | Action | Time |
|------|--------|------|
| **Immediate** | Close book, say it aloud | 30 sec |
| **+1 hour** | Read MY sentence, recall | 2 min |
| **+3 hours** | Say concept while walking | 1 min |
| **Before sleep** | Review ALL sentences from today aloud | 5 min |
| **Next day** | Explain each sentence without looking | 5 min |
| **+3 days** | Quick vocal recall | 3 min |
| **+1 week** | 2-minute check | 2 min |
| **+1 month** | Final check | 1 min |

> **The first 4 recalls are NON-NEGOTIABLE.** They are the difference between remembering for 1 day vs remembering for 1 year.
