# The 5-Step Method

For ANY subject, no matter how hard.

1. **BREAK** — Take only 2 minutes worth. Cover the rest with paper. One chunk = your working memory can handle it.

2. **READ ALOUD + SIMPLIFY** — Say it like to a 10-year-old. If you can't simplify it, you didn't understand it.

3. **CLOSE + RECALL** — Stand up. Walk. Say it without looking. Struggle for 10 seconds before peeking. The pain of not remembering IS the moment you learn.

4. **WRITE ONE SENTENCE** — From memory, not copying. This becomes YOUR note. The paper is your extended brain.

5. **REPEAT AFTER 10 MINUTES** — Look at your sentence. Still there? If not, redo from Step 2.

> **After Step 5, write a note in this system.** That note is your external memory. Over time, these notes become your personal textbook — written in YOUR words, linked by YOUR understanding.
